My name is Christine Than. I have always enjoy customizing and creating a new feel and look for websites. Apart of this project was to create and design a small fun game. I created a Battlestar Galactica Matching Card Game Theme.

For those who do not know what a Matching Card Game is, I will briefly paraphrase the game. A Matching card is a game that requires mental stamina and concentration. The game itself can be a multiplayer game. However, my game requires only one. A player only has one minute to finish matching all 18 cards. If the player does not finish matching the cards within a minute, the player will lose.If the player finishes matching all 18 cards within the one minute interval, he or she wins.
